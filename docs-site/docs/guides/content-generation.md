# Content Generation

Iqonga includes AI-powered content tools you can use from the dashboard and in workflows.

## Content types

- **Social posts** – Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn
- **Long-form** – Blogs, newsletters, articles, whitepapers
- **Marketing** – Copy, ads, product descriptions, reviews
- **Creative** – Stories, books, poems, screenplays
- **eBooks** – Multi-format exports, audiobooks, publishing
- **Content briefs** – Audience analysis, SEO keywords, competitor insights
- **Multi-modal** – Text + images + video in one package
- **Content series** – Multi-piece campaigns with templates
- **Repurposing** – Convert content to multiple platform formats

## Media

- **Images** – AI image generation and editing (background removal, upscaling, logo maker)
- **Video** – Veo, Runway, HeyGen avatar videos
- **Music & lyrics** – AI music and lyrics generation

## Scheduling & planning

- **Scheduled posting** – Automate distribution; set frequency and platforms.
- **Visual content calendar** – Drag-and-drop planning and calendar view.
- **Content optimization** – Readability, SEO, and engagement suggestions.
- **Performance prediction** – Engagement scores, viral potential, best time to post.

Use [Workflows](/docs/guides/workflows) to chain content generation with agents, approvals, and publishing steps.
